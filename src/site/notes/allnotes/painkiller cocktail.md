# Ingredients
- orange juice 1 oz
- cream of coconut 1 oz
	- if using from a can, stir contents well before portioning
- pineapple juice 4 oz
- rum 2 oz
- nutmeg for garnish

# Directions
- combine into cocktail shaker with ice and shake
- open pour into double rocks glass
- garnish with nutmeg

 - add mint garnish?
