#recipe 

# Ingredients
#ingredients 

## pork
* [ ] pork shoulder 2 lbs
	* get 4lb if plan to only keep the meat after cooking
	* chunk into 2 inch pieces
* [ ] orange 1
* [ ] lime 1
* [ ] cumin 2 tbsp
* [ ] oregano 2 tbsp
* [ ] bay leaves 3
* [ ] chicken broth 1 box
* [ ] chipotle peppers 1 can
* [ ] root beer 1 bottle
* [ ] soy sauce 1 tbsp
* [ ] yellow onion 1
* [ ] garlic cloves 4

## tacos
- [ ] tortillas
- [ ] radishes
- [ ] cheese (cojita, jack, etc)


# Directions

## in the morning, start the pork
 - combine in the slow cooker on low heat:
	 - orange zest
	 - orange juice
	 - lime juice
	 - cumin
	 - oregano
	 - chipotle peppers
	 - root beer
	 - soy sauce
	 - onion (roughly diced)
	 - garlic (roughly diced)
	 - bay leaves
	 - pork
 - add chicken broth to just cover all ingredients
 - cook on low until at least 145 F, about 6 hours minimum

## prep tacos
 - thinly slice radishes
 - heat tortillas on griddle
 - combine radishes, cheese, pork
