# Ingredients
#ingredients 
* [ ] 2 oz rye whiskey (or other)
* [ ] 1 oz lemon juice
* [ ] 3/4 oz maple syrup
* [ ] 1 egg white
* [ ] dry red wine


# Directions

 - combine whiskey, lemon juice, maple syrup, and egg white
 - dry shake
 - add ice, shake again
 - strain into empty, cold rocks glass
 - top with ~1 oz dry red wine
